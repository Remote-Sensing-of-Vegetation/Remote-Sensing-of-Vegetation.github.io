This is a blog for Remote Sensing of Vegetation methods. The main focus is in R and Python scripts.

Authors

Javier Lopatin (Admin) is a PhD student at the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT), Germany. His project use UAV-hyperspectral imagery for the monitoring of Wetlands and Grasslands ecological status. He also works modeling LiDAR data to predict biodiversity in complex forest.
