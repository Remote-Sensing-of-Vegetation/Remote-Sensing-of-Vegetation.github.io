Authors

Javier Lopatin (Admin) is a PhD student at the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT). His project use UAV-based hyperspectral data to answer ecological questions related to grasslands and wetlands. He also works modeling LiDAR data to predict biodiversity in complex forest.
